Project Name: SafeBite
App Description (474 characters)

SafeBite is a smart gluten-free companion app designed to help users make confident food choices every day.
From browsing curated safe recipes to verifying packaged products in real time, the app simplifies healthy living. 
Users can explore trusted restaurants, bakeries, and markets across Jordan, discover verified brands, and unlock exclusive in-app rewards 
all within a clean, intuitive, and performance-focused mobile experience.

Overview
SafeBite is a comprehensive gluten-free lifestyle platform built to eliminate uncertainty around food safety. 
The app combines recipe discovery, real-time product verification powered by Open Food Facts, location-based restaurant exploration, 
and curated safe brands. With secure authentication and an engaging rewards system, it delivers reliability, convenience, and trust in one 
seamless digital solution.

Carefully Executed Features
1. Real-Time Product Safety Checks
Integrated with Open Food Facts to instantly validate ingredients and determine whether products are safe or not.

2. Verified Safe Locations
Explore trusted restaurants, bakeries, and markets with map-based navigation for easy and secure dining choices.

3. Curated Gluten-Free Cookbook
A categorized recipe library featuring safe, easy-to-follow meal options tailored to gluten-sensitive users.

4. In-App Rewards & Promotions
Built-in coupon system offering exclusive discounts to enhance user engagement and long-term retention.
