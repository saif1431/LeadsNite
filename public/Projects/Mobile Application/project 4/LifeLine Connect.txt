Overview
LifeLine Connect is a comprehensive digital emergency coordination solution built to simplify and accelerate medical assistance. 
The app allows users to report emergencies with one tap, discover nearby hospitals, access daily health resources, and securely manage personal 
medical information such as blood group and emergency contacts. A dedicated volunteer dashboard ensures real-time case visibility and faster 
response, strengthening community-driven lifesaving support.
