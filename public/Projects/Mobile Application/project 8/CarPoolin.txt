CarPoolin is a modern ride-sharing platform designed to make everyday travel affordable, 
convenient, and community-driven. Users can quickly search routes, select travel dates, choose 
passenger counts, and connect with verified drivers in seconds. With a clean interface and seamless 
booking flow, the app simplifies intercity commuting while helping users save money and reduce travel costs efficiently.

Overview
CarPoolin connects drivers and passengers through a streamlined carpooling experience. Built with a minimal, 
user-friendly interface, it allows travelers to search routes, compare ride timings and prices, and book seats instantly. 
The platform encourages cost-effective travel while optimizing unused car seats, making commuting smarter and more accessible.
